Play the Simon Game!

Instructions:

The Simon Game is an old game that requires you to match the colors in the order that they light up. They game gets progressively harder, incrementing by one color in the chain (at random) until you fail or you win. You win by successfully matching 20 colors in a row.

You can also enable Strict Mode. Under regular circumstances, if you mess up the order, you can retry to match the sequence. In strict mode, messing up resets the game to 1. Good luck!
